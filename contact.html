Frequently Asked Questions

How can I support your ministry?

We appreciate your interest in supporting our ministry! You can partner with us through prayer, volunteering, or financial giving. Visit our Give page for more information.

Do you offer counseling services?

While we don't provide professional counseling, we do offer biblical guidance and prayer support. For serious issues, we recommend connecting with a licensed Christian counselor in your area.

Can I request a speaker for my event?

Yes! Our team members are available to speak at churches and events when schedules permit. Please use the contact form to inquire about availability.

How do I access your resources?

All of our free resources are available in the Learn section of our website. For premium resources, visit our online store.
